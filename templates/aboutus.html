AI4CE

The AI4CE (pronounced as “A-I-force”) lab at New York University conducts multidisciplinary use-inspired research. We aim to advance fundamental automation and intelligence technologies such as robot vision and machine learning, while addressing challenges of their applications in civil and mechanical engineering domains.
